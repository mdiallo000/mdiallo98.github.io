Movie Land

This project forced me to use a lot of the new concepts i learned in react. Browser Router, Routes, Params as well as Hooks. Using the movie database a user can look for a movie that they have been comtemplating to watch and get more information on the film. Its a nice alternative to googling a film and clicking through multiple pages to get the info that you want. Everything is centralized here.
